* [Deep Learning: A Practitioner's Approach, by Josh Patterson and Adam Gibson](https://www.amazon.com/Deep-Learning-Practitioners-Josh-Patterson/dp/1491914254/)
* [Neural Networks and Deep Learning, by Michael Nielsen](http://neuralnetworksanddeeplearning.com/)
* [Deep Learning, Ian Goodfellow and Yoshua Bengio](https://www.amazon.com/Deep-Learning-NONE-Ian-Goodfellow-ebook/dp/B01MRVFGX4/)
* [Deep Learning with Python, by Francois Chollet](https://www.amazon.com/Deep-Learning-Python-Francois-Chollet/dp/1617294438/)
* [The Master Algorithm: How the Quest for the Ultimate Learning Machine Will Remake Our World, by Pedro Domingos](https://www.amazon.com/Master-Algorithm-Ultimate-Learning-Machine-ebook/dp/B06XK8D37J/)
* [Machine Learning with Python Cookbook: Practical Solutions from Preprocessing to Deep Learning, by Chris Albon](https://www.amazon.com/Machine-Learning-Python-Cookbook-Preprocessing/dp/1491989386/)

* [Python Machine Learning: Machine Learning and Deep Learning with Python, scikit-learn, and TensorFlow, by Sebastian Raschka](https://www.amazon.com/Python-Machine-Learning-scikit-learn-TensorFlow-ebook/dp/B0742K7HYF/)
